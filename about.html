Pork Siopao

Recipe: 1 tbsp Active Dry Yeast 1 tbsp Baking Powder 1 tbsp White Sugar 3 tbsp Shortening 1/4 cup White Sugar 5 cups All Purpose Flour Warm Water

INSTRUCTIONS Pour the yeast and 1 tbsp white suger in the warm water. Stir well and let stand for 5 minutes until yeast activates. Set aside. In a mixing bowl, add flour, 1/4 cup sugar and yeast mixture. Mix. Add baking powder and shortening. Mix until dough is formed. On a flat surface, sprinkle flour and transfer the dough. Knead the dough for 8 to 10 minutes or until soft. Form into a ball and cover with oil. Place in a bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Set aside and wait until it rises. Remove wrap and punch down. On a floured surface, roll and cut into half then slice into pieces. Flatten into 5 inches circle leaving a bulge in the middle of the dough. Add pork asado filling and seal/enclose the flattened dough. Place siopao in the steamer and steam for 10 to 12 minutes. Serve and enjoy! Oyster sauce can be use as sauce.
